About Us

Zila parishad kendriya prathmik shala Lasurgaon is a group of schools consisting of kindergarten, and primary school located at Lasurgaon, vaijapur, chhatrapati sambhajinagar, maharashtra

**It was founded by Prasad and was established in 2006.****

Zila parishad kendriya prathmik shala Lasurgaon, set on hundreds of square meters of lush green land in the Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, is a breath of fresh air. The school has all that parents need to ensure qualitative, affordable and morally sound education for their children.
